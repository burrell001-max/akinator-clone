Last updated: June 2026

MindGenie AI ("we", "us", "our") operates the website mindgenieai.xyz (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you visit and play the game on this Site, and how that information is used.

Information We Collect

MindGenie AI does not require you to create an account, and we do not knowingly collect names, email addresses, or other personally identifying information in order for you to play the game.

When you visit the Site, the following information may be collected automatically:

  • Usage and analytics data — such as page views and approximate visit counts, collected via GoatCounter, a privacy-focused analytics tool, and Google Analytics. This may include your browser type, general device type, approximate location, referring page, and an anonymized or hashed identifier. Google Analytics uses cookies to help us understand how visitors use the Site; you can learn more or opt out via Google's Ads Settings or by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
  • Gameplay session data — when you play, your answers (e.g. "Yes"/"No"/"Probably") are sent to our game server to generate the next question and final guess. This data is tied to a temporary, randomly generated session ID and is used only to run that single round of the game.

Children's Privacy

This Site is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.
