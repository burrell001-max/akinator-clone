About MindGenie AI

MindGenie AI is an independent, free-to-play browser game inspired by classic mind-reading guessing games like 20 Questions. Pick any character in your head, answer a short series of simple questions, and watch our guessing engine try to figure out who you were thinking of.

Why We Built It

We wanted a lightweight, no-sign-up way to enjoy a quick guessing game from any device — no installs, no accounts, just a quick browser-based round whenever you have a couple of minutes to kill.

How It Works

Each round, your yes/no/maybe answers are sent to our question-and-guessing engine, which narrows down the field of possible characters with every answer until it's ready to make its best guess. It isn't perfect — sometimes it guesses wrong, especially with very obscure characters — but that's part of the fun of playing.

Who's Behind This

MindGenie AI is an independently run hobby project. We're a small team focused on building simple, free browser games. You can reach us any time using the details on our Contact page.
