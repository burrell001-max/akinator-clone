Why does the game say it's "waking up" sometimes?

Our guessing engine runs on a free-tier server that goes to sleep when it isn't being used. The first game of the day can take up to 30 seconds to "wake up" — after that, rounds load quickly.

About the Genie

MindGenie AI is a free browser-based guessing game inspired by classic "20 questions" style mind-reading games. Think of any character — real, fictional, historical, or from pop culture — and the Genie will ask you a short series of simple yes-or-no style questions. Using your answers, our guessing engine narrows down the possibilities and reveals its best guess at who you were thinking of.

The game was built as a fun, lightweight way to test how well a simple question-and-answer model can "read your mind." No account, download, or payment is required — just open the page and start playing in your browser, on desktop or mobile.

Every round you play also helps the Genie learn. When you rate whether its final guess was right or wrong, that feedback quietly improves how it asks questions in future games.

How to Play
  1. Think of any character — a person, fictional hero, villain, celebrity, or historical figure.
  2. Click "Start Playing" to begin a new round.
  3. Answer each question with Yes, No, Probably, Probably not, or I'm not sure.
  4. Keep answering as the Genie asks more questions and narrows down its guess.
  5. After a series of questions, the Genie reveals who it thinks you were picturing.
  6. Click "Challenge Me Again" to play another round with a new character in mind.

The more honestly and consistently you answer, the better the Genie's guess will be. Some rounds it gets it right away — others it takes a wrong guess and learns from the round.
